Τήνος

Marble villages, dovecotes, and the Cyclades' spiritual heart

Συνολική βαθμολογία: 3.7/5 · 194 km² · 8600 κάτοικοι

Tinos is the Cyclades' spiritual heart — home to the Evangelistria church, the most important pilgrimage site in Greek Orthodoxy. Beyond the pilgrimage, the island hides 40 immaculate marble-paved villages built against granite hills, white dovecotes dotting terraced valleys, and some of the best food in the Cyclades. It escaped mass tourism because everyone was rushing to Mykonos next door.

Δρομολόγιο 3 ημερών — Τήνος

Μέρα 1: Chora & the South

Διανυκτέρευση: Tinos Town · Οδήγηση: 25 km, ~45 min

  1. 09:00 · Tinos Town (Chora)
    Port town with marble-paved alleys. Walk up the main pedestrian street leading to the Evangelistria church.
  2. 10:00 · Panagia Evangelistria
    The most important Orthodox pilgrimage site in Greece. The miraculous icon was found here in 1822. Visit even if you're not religious — the marble architecture is stunning.
  3. 12:30 · Agios Fokas Beach
    Sandy beach 2 km south of Chora with tavernas. Calm water, family-friendly.
  4. 14:00 · Lunch — Tarsanas
    Seafood taverna in the harbor. Fresh fish, carafes of tsipouro, the quintessential Tinian experience.
  5. 16:30 · Kionia Beach
    Long pebble beach with ancient ruins nearby. Sunset views back toward Chora.

Μέρα 2: The Mountain Villages

Διανυκτέρευση: Tinos Town · Οδήγηση: 55 km, ~110 min

  1. 10:00 · Pyrgos
    The most beautiful marble village in Greece. Birthplace of Greece's greatest sculptors. Marble everything: fountains, benches, doorframes.
  2. 11:30 · Museum of Marble Crafts
    Small but essential museum showing Tinos' marble-carving tradition, which still produces master craftsmen.
  3. 13:00 · Panormos Port
    Tiny fishing harbor 5 min from Pyrgos. Lunch at one of two tavernas on the water.
  4. 16:00 · Volax
    Village surrounded by an otherworldly field of massive round granite boulders. Walk the short loop around the village.
  5. 20:00 · Dinner — Metaxi Mas
    Modern Tinian cuisine in Chora. Book ahead — tables fill fast.

Μέρα 3: The Dovecote Valleys

Διανυκτέρευση: Departure · Οδήγηση: 40 km, ~90 min

  1. 10:00 · Tarabados (dovecotes)
    Valley with 50+ traditional dovecotes — intricate marble lattice-work. Walk the 1 km trail through the terraces.
  2. 11:30 · Kardiani
    Stunning cliff-hugging village with panoramic views over the Aegean. Quiet, very local.
  3. 14:00 · Livada Beach
    Wild beach on the windward east coast. Strong winds in July-August, perfect for kitesurfing.
  4. 17:00 · Back to Tinos Town
    Return to port for your ferry. Quick stop at Lehrni cafe for a frappé before leaving.
