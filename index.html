Why this site exists

Choosing a Greek island shouldn’t feel like a research project.

There are too many options, too many opinions, and too much noise. You end up comparing tabs for hours and still second-guessing the decision. This site cuts through that.

Start with your priorities, not a search engine.

Select what you care about, and the site points you to the islands that actually fit. No overthinking, no endless comparisons.

📍 Where to stay
🏛️ What to see

No extra research needed.

The Point

This isn’t about inspiration. It’s about making a clear decision fast—and having a solid plan immediately after.

Because planning a trip shouldn’t take longer than the trip itself.
