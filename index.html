Perfect Pairings

Milos + Kimolos

A 20-minute local ferry runs multiple times daily from Pollonia (Milos). Perfect for moonscape beaches and authentic seclusion.

Paros + Naxos

Only 45 minutes by ferry. High-end nightlife meets endless sandy coastlines. The best-connected pair in the Aegean.
