Perfect Pairings

Maximize your trip by combining islands that are geographically close and share frequent ferry connections.

Milos + Kimolos

The Vibe: Volcanic moonscapes meet authentic seclusion.

The Link: A 20-minute local ferry (Pantofla) runs multiple times daily from Pollonia. No booking required.

Paros + Naxos

The Vibe: The Cycladic heart. High-end nightlife meets endless sandy coastlines.

The Link: 45 minutes by ferry. These are the most connected islands in the Aegean.

Skiathos + Skopelos

The Vibe: Lush pine forests and the 'Mamma Mia' backdrop.

The Link: 30-60 minutes depending on the boat. Perfect for those who love green landscapes over dry rocks.
