Why this site exists

For travellers who want to live the holiday, not plan it.

This site is for people who don't want choices. They want the gist, without the fluff.

Greece has 83 inhabited islands and countless guides, blogs and listicles trying to cover them all. It's overwhelming, and most of it is shallow.

Stergios Gousios
Founder · Athens

Aegean Blueprint is made by locals who have actually travelled to the islands on the site — not curated from search rankings, and not stitched together by AI. Each island page gives you one clear, opinionated recommendation: what to see, where to swim, how long to stay. That's it.

The point
